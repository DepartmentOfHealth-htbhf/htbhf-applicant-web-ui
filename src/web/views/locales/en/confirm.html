What happens next

1. Your prepaid card will be sent

We will send your prepaid card to the address you gave us.

2. Money will be added to your card

We will add money to your card every 4 weeks so that you can buy healthy food and milk. You will get £12.40 every 4 weeks until your baby is born.

3. You can use your card to pay for fruit, vegetables and milk

You can find out how to use your prepaid card and what you can buy with your money on GOV.UK (link opens in a new tab)

! Warning When your baby is born you must telephone 07448 896 279 to tell us. We will then increase your money to £6.20 each week until your baby is 1 year old.
